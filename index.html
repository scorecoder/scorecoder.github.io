Hi! I am Vedant!
Welcome to my site!

My name is Vedant Nanda. I live in Chennai, India.

I am in 8th standard, and go to SBOA Higher Secondary school.
With the help of my father, I have created this site to stay in touch
with my friends and make new friends from around the world.

My hobbies are painting, music, singing, acting and participating in spell bee
competitions.

I am learning keyboard and harmoniam, both traiditional Indian style
called Carnatic.

I love making new friends. If you happen to come by my site, I hope you
enjoy and leave some comments about you.
